# Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

## Introduction
**Spaghetti Carbonara** is a traditional Italian pasta dish known for its rich, creamy texture *with out using cream*.
It relies on simple ingredients and precise technique.

> Simple ingredients. Big flavor. Timeless comfort food.

---
## Recipe Overview

- **Cuisine:** Italian  
- **Prep Time:** *10 minutes*  
- **Cook Time:** *15 minutes*  
- **Total Time:** *25 minutes* 
---

## Ingredients
### Main Ingredients
- 200g **spaghetti**
- 100g **pancetta** or **bacon**, diced
- 2 large **eggs**
- 50g **Parmesan cheese**, finely grated
### Seasoning
- Freshly ground **black pepper**
- **Salt** (for pasta water)
---

## Instructions

### Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

1. **Boil the Pasta**
   - Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil.
   - Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until *al dente*.

2. **Prepare the Sauce**
   - In a bowl, whisk together:
     - Eggs  
     - Grated Parmesan  
     - A generous amount of black pepper  

3. **Cook the Pancetta**
   - Heat a pan over medium heat.
   - Cook pancetta until crispy.
   - Add garlic if using, then remove from heat.

4. **Combine Everything**
   - Drain pasta (reserve some pasta water).
   - Add hot pasta to the pan with pancetta.
   - Remove from heat and stir in egg mixture quickly.
   - Add pasta water **gradually** if needed.
