Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and clack pepper. Creamy, comforting, and incredibly simple.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti 200g
  • pancetta and bacon 150g each
  • eggs 1
  • cheddar cheese 50g
  • black pepper
  • salt 3g

Instructions

  • Boil salted water and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  • Whisk eggs, cheese, and black pepper in a bowl.
  • Cook pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  • Add hot pasta to the pan and remove from heat.
  • Stir in egg mixture quickly to form a sauce.
