Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. Incredibly creamy, comforting, and surprisingly simple to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or bacon
  • 1 egg
  • 50g cheddar cheese
  • Fresh black pepper
  • 3g salt

Instructions

  1. Boil salted water and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Whisk eggs, cheese, and black pepper in a bowl.
  3. Cook pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  4. Add hot pasta to the pan and remove from heat.
  5. Stir in the egg mixture swiftly to form a creamy sauce.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 min

Cook Time: 15 min

Difficulty: Easy

Servings: 2
