About Me

I started learning Chinese calligraphy when I was three years old, and there is something that has been with me for fifteen years. It witnessed my very first calligraphy class, the very first character I wrote, and even my very first little piece of work.

I used to practice it for at least one hour every day, whether at school or at home. Over time, calligraphy simply became part of my life. By the time I graduated from elementary school, I even held my own calligraphy exhibition and invited the principal to visit.

Calligraphy not only connects me with traditional Chinese culture, but also taught me persistence and focus.
